For the students who have Computer Aided Education course registered, the Midterm deadline is **14 Nov 2025** for project submission via email.

Send your PowerPoint project that has to include at least the following:

- Has to be on an educational academic topic.
- Has to work in the PowerPoint Kiosk presentation mode (it means you have to build all functionality and navigation yourself).
- Has to be readable and follow the principle of content chunking.
- Has to have organized text and font use (for headings, subheadings, body content, feedback, annotations, etc.).
- Must have at least 20 slides.
