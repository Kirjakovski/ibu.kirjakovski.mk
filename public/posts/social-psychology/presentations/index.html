SOCIAL PSYCHOLOGY
04 Apr 2026

To achieve activity points for the course, you must identify a peer-reviewed social psychology study—via your textbook or Google Scholar—and distill its essence into a sharp, two-minute rapid oral presentation. Because no visual aids or notes are permitted, you must rely entirely on your verbal delivery to hook your audience, explain the experimental manipulation, and communicate the significance of the findings. You need to clearly explain the research question the study addresses, the hypotheses, the methodology, and the conclusions.
